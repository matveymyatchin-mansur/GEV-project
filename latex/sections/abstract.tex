\begin{abstract}
    This is a personal engineering research study, conducted by Matvey Myatchin, into Ground Effect Vehicles (GEVs). This study builds upon preliminary airfoil investigations conducted by the author during a prior academic project, which motivated the selection of the present baseline airfoils. Using CFD simulations, this research aims to evaluate and compare the aerodynamic performance of various airfoils in ground effect conditions, with the ultimate goal of identifying the most suitable airfoil for a GEV design.
\end{abstract}
