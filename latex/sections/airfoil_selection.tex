\subsection{Airfoil selection}
\label{sec:airfoil-selection}

In this section I will discuss several prospective airfoil for the ground effect vehicle. As established previously in the literature study, the DHMTU class of airfoils was specifically designed for GEVs, yet whether it actually outperforms other more conventional airfoils, is yet to be seen. For this purpose, 3 airfoils will be selected for testing and comparison: The DHMTU xxx series airfoil, the NACA 6-series airfoil, and the Horton-Brothers HR-1 airfoil. %Exact airfoils to be determined at a later date.
